Our Core Projects

At Marine Soul Trust, we are committed to actionable conservation. Below are our primary focus areas.

Sea Turtles

We actively patrol nesting grounds to protect hatchlings from predators and human interference, while coordinating beach cleanups to remove hazardous microplastics.

Coral Reefs

We manage active coral nursery programs where we grow climate-resilient coral fragments in controlled environments before transplanting them onto degraded reef systems.

Whale Protection

We monitor seasonal migration patterns. Our advocacy team works with shipping authorities to implement "slow-down" zones, reducing the risk of vessel strikes and noise pollution.
