About Our Organisation

founded in 2018, Marine Soul Trust was born out of the passion of marine biologists and ocean lovers in Muizenberg, Cape Town. Initially a volunteer-driven beach cleanup effort tackling plastic pollution in False Bay , it earned official NPO status in 2020, which paved the way for coral nursery projects and structured community education .

Our Mission

Marine Soul Trust is a dedicated NGO working to ensure that sea creatures from the smallest plankton to the largest blue whale have a safe,plastic-free environment to thrive in. We srtrive to be the bridge between human activity and ocean health
